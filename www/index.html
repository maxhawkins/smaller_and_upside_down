Smaller & Upside Down

April 9-11 2015 · San Francisco, CA

Smaller & Upside Down is an arcade of 3D-printed lenses that distort faces

The project makes its public debut April 9-11, 2015 on San Francisco's Market Street. Join us there, or scroll down to learn how the lenses are made.
